Daily Drinking Limit

Setting a daily limit on how many drinks you can have is an effective way to ensure safer drinking. This doesnt mean you should aim to drink this many daily, but to try not to exceed it.

Dry Days

Continous, long term drinking contributes to health problems in the same way that binge drinking does. Having days you choose not to drink is a surefire way to prevent this.
